shopping london's authentic'spirit of place'

A ground breaking duty free store at London Luton Airport designed with the innovative theme ‘It’s a London thing’
We understood that ‘shopping London’ is important to the airport’s PAX demographic. A typically low cost, younger, European profile who find fascination in London’s retail variety. And so did we, from the flagship encrusted Regent Street to the buzzing boutiques of Shoreditch, the design is inspired by London’s shopping.
We know that a rich and authentically crafted ‘Spirit of Place’ can be a key experience driver for all retail, especially airports.
Designed for Lagardère Travel retail Services the shopping experience captures the diversity and creative energy of London shopping.
As many airport stores tend to be similar, we injected a strong London personality into the shopping experience. Edgy, urban elements such as exposed brickwork and graffiti in some areas, contrasted with a highly, polished premium feel in other areas such as cosmetics. Expressing the spirit of London in a unique, appealing and sometimes slightly irreverent way.
An eyecatching ceiling disc featuring a ‘punk-ed’ Union Jack flag provides a focal stage for an assortment of English brands. A showcase for English brands, from new players, to boutique offers and trusted favourites. This programmed offer pumps in the entrepreneurial buzz, newness and refreshment of the London shopping experience.
“I am very proud to be a part of this exceptional new Aelia Duty Free opening at London Luton Airport. As the largest store in Lagardère Travel Retail’s global portfolio, we are thrilled to have opened such an inspiring and invigorating retail facility.” Amaury Dehen General director of store operators Lagardère Travel retail UK
