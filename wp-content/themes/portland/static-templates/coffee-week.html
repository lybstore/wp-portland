using coffee to do good

A brand campaign to wake up the nation using #spreadthesmile

UK Coffee Week is a nationwide fundraising initiative to bring clean water to communities that grow coffee. Our challenge was to create a campaign that would inspire coffee shops and coffee drinkers to raise funds for this good cause.

Using #spreadthesmile, coffee lovers could raise awareness online, while we created a warm brand that coffee shops could embrace, to promote giving 5p per cup of coffee sold to help change lives in Tanzania, Rwanda and Ethiopia.
